ARMA Models & Stationarity
Enders Ch. 1–2 · fpp3 Ch. 1–3 · forecast / feasts
~4h▶
Reading

  • fpp3 Ch. 3 — ACF and PACF in the tidy framework.
  • Hamilton Ch. 3 — AR(p)/MA(q) lag polynomial notation. Skim derivations.
Diagnostic rule: AR(p) — ACF decays geometrically, PACF cuts off at lag p. MA(q) — ACF cuts off at lag q, PACF decays. Know this cold before coding.
Exercises

# auto.arima(ar1, ic="aic"); auto.arima(ma1, ic="bic")
